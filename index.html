About

Our project

dePLY was founded with technology and ambition at heart, using these, we are out to reinvent the way furniture is designed, manufactured and bought. We are a duo of highly creative designers from various backgrounds who share one common objective.

Local

Our minimal and simple supply chain allows us to manufacture with no transport or material warehousing costs, eliminating the risk of any overstocking. Due to the streamlined manufacturing processes, minimal space is required. Allowing local and direct delivery to our customers.

Manufacture

Using our digitalised designs, and computer assisted technology, we are able to create a micro manufacturing workspace at the point of sale location. Minor modifications and customizations can be implemented rapidly without compromising lead time.

Designs

Our designs are all computer generated and begin at version 1.0, to meet the needs of customers and to provide an bespoke furniture experience, we are happy to change and manipulate our version 1.0 as many times as it takes to satisfy your needs.

Our products have not only been designed to look aesthetically pleasing, our products are designed to be assembled in minutes, with no tools and minimal instructions.
